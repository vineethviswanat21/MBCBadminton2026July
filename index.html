Tournament Notes

Tap any rule to expand it. Please review all notes before the tournament begins.

Before the start of every match, each team will be given 45 seconds to discuss strategy and prepare for the game.
Each team is entitled to one 45-second interval during a match. The interval may only be taken at the designated break:
  • 11 points in a 21-point game
  • 8 points in a 15-point game
This break may be used for strategy discussions, hydration and brief recovery.
A 2-minute break will be provided between each game across all stages of the tournament.
Standard badminton service rules will be followed. Players will receive a fair opportunity if an incorrect serve is made. If the same service fault is repeated consecutively, the player will lose the right to serve and the opponent will be awarded the point.
As this is a friendly tournament, the 0–7 mercy rule will not be enforced. Every match will continue until the final point.
Lingmei 90 Pro is the official shuttlecock. Each team is responsible for bringing approved shuttlecocks.
Once a team has registered, participation is expected. Teams that withdraw, fail to report or do not participate without prior approval will automatically be placed in the bottom four positions of the final standings.
An injured team will receive one injury timeout of up to 3–5 minutes. If the player cannot continue after the allotted time, the organizing committee may declare the team retired from the match or tournament, as appropriate.
Player substitutions will not be permitted once the tournament has commenced.
The umpire's decision shall be final and binding on all matters related to the tournament.
The tournament will generally follow Badminton World Federation rules and regulations. As this is a friendly competition, all participants are expected to:
  • Demonstrate good sportsmanship.
  • Respect fellow players, umpires and organizers.
  • Play with honesty, fairness and in the true spirit of the game.
